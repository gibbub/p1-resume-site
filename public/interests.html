Interests

Illustration & Design

I loved to draw since I was a child. Making art is something I take seriously, but is also something I have fun with and enjoy greatly!
Visual storytelling is what my love of art and design boils down to. Graphic novels and animation are the most literal form of visual storytelling, but even a website or logo design can convey a story or message to its user, and this is what I find most interesting about design as a whole.
Color, shape, placement of elements, and other visual aspects of a design can change so much about how a user interacts and interprets that design, and I love to explore and experiment with such aspects.
